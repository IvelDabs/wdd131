Project Site Plan

Site Name

Site Name: Mountain Explorer

Reason for Selection: The name reflects the focus of the site on outdoor adventure and exploration in mountainous regions.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide detailed information about hiking trails, gear recommendations, and membership benefits for an outdoor adventure club dedicated to exploring mountainous terrains. It will include trail maps, tips for explorers, and a sign-up form for new members.

Scenarios

  • What are the best hiking trails for beginners in the Rocky Mountains?
  • How can I become a member of the Mountain Explorer Club?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2d3e50 (Dark Blue) – Used for headings, primary buttons, and navigation bar.

Secondary Color: #f4a261 (Soft Orange) – Used for accents, buttons, and hover effects.

Typography

Primary Font: Arial, sans-serif – Used for the body text.

Secondary Font: 'Georgia', serif – Used for headings.
